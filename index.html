About us

Our NGO "FEED INDIA" is feeding poor people all over india. Across many states mumbai, delhi, kashmir and many more. We give 300 RS food to one family which include 3kg rice, various dals, soap, and much more every month. We get donations for gulf countries also such as Saudi Arabia, UAE, etc. We organise food packages and goes to area where their are natural calamities occurs like flood, earthquake, etc and distribute there.
